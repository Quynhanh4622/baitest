Ba Vì NATIONAL PARKS

Coming to Ba Vi National Park, visitors will be immersed in the majestic natural scenery of mountains and forests, returning to the legend of Son Tinh - Thuy Tinh is sacred but poetic.

Address: Tan Linh Commune, Ba Vi, Hanoi
Tel: (84-4) 33881205 ; Hotline: 0976380979
E: festival.com
 

Information Introduction

Located about 50km from the center of the capital to the West, with a fresh and cool mountain climate, Ba Vi National Park has long been an ideal destination for domestic and foreign tourists. Coming to Ba Vi National Park, visitors will be immersed in the majestic natural scenery of mountains and forests, returning to the legend of Son Tinh - Thuy Tinh is a sacred but poetic nature.

In the middle of the semi-mountainous region of Doai, there is a tall mountain with 3 peaks that make up the name Ba Vi, that is King Peak 1,296m high, Tan Vien peak 1,227m high, Ngoc Hoa peak 1,131m high. . In the middle of the mountain, there are many waterfalls pouring down day and night, forming attractive tourist spots such as Khoang Xanh, Ao Vua, Nga Waterfall, Suoi Ngoc ..., all forming a charming mountain population.Ba Vi National Park is located in that massive mountain, extending to the top of Vien Nam in Hoa Binh land.

 

Ba Vi National Park has 1,209 species of flora, of which 21 species of rare plants are listed in the Red Book of Vietnam such as: Bach Xanh, Honey Sick, Three-Nose Hazel, White-striped Pine, Fairy Flower, Lobster Beard, Kim The fauna has 63 species of mammals, with many rare species such as: Palm Civet, Big Cu, White pheasant, Earth Dragon, Ca cuong, White-tailed Dragon Butterfly ...

 

Ba Vi Mountain is said to be the incarnation of the Holy Ghost Tan Vien Son Tinh, the top saint of the Four Immortals in the minds of Vietnamese people. In order to commemorate the water power of the Holy Ghost Tan Vien, people set up His Temple on top of Tan Vien, customarily called Thuong Temple. Thuong Temple was ranked as a national-level historical and cultural monument in February 2008. In addition to historical values, visitors will have the opportunity to learn more about the culture of Lang of the 18th King Hung dynasty and feel more deeply about the love between Son Tinh guy and princess Ngoc Hoa.


On the top of the king is the Temple of President Ho Chi Minh, an excellent son of the Vietnamese nation, a world cultural celebrity. The temple is located in the midst of a magical space, mixed in floating white clouds and the sound of wild birds lined under tall trees.


At the height of 600m, it is a historical revolutionary relic that marks the brave battle between our army and the French colonialists in the Hoa Binh campaign in 1952. Visiting this place, visitors are reliving the heroic past. be in love with the spirit of Tan mountain Tan river.


Coming to Ba Vi National Park, visitors can unleash exploring with thoughtful and dedicated tours:
 

- Route 1: Cactus garden, bamboo garden, national coconut palm - 400m altitude tourist area - Tan Vien peak - king top (Holy Duc Tan Vien Temple, Ho Chi Minh Temple).


- Route 2: National cactus garden, bamboo, palm tree - 400m altitude tourist area - 600m high revolutionary historical relic area - 800m high French villa ruins.


- Route 3: Trekking to visit the primeval forest, the thousand-year-old Bach Xanh and Bach Xanh complex.
